About Maruti Engineers

Maruti Engineers started in 2005 from the small town of Rajula with a clear goal: deliver dependable, safe, and professional demolition work.

Over the years, we expanded our business to key locations including Ahmedabad, Gandhinagar, Gandhidham, and nearby regions. This growth has come from consistent project delivery and trust built with clients.

From 2026 onwards, we are also taking up construction projects to provide broader end-to-end services. While our legacy is in demolition, our next chapter is focused on building strong structures and long-term partnerships.
